About the Dashboard:

The Belt and Road Initiative, launched in 2013, has been a transformative force in global infrastructure, particularly in developing nations. By leveraging an open-source database, I examined China's investments worldwide, with a specific focus on Pakistan up until 2018. The resulting insights shed light on the impact of this initiative on Pakistan in comparison to other regions. This analysis was particularly significant as it coincided with the 5th anniversary of the Belt and Road initiative, which was significantly mediatised and it was essential to analyse the real impact of the initiative while comparing it with the media narrative.
